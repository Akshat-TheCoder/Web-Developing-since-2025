Electrolysis of Water

  • Overall reaction :

    2H 2O(l) electrolysis → 2H 2(g) + O 2(g)

  • At the Cathode (reduction) :

    2H 2O(l) + 2e - → H 2(g) + 2OH -(aq)

  • At the Anode (oxidation) :

    2H 2O(l) → O 2(g) +4H +(aq) + 4e - (in acidic solution)

Electrolysis of molten sodium chloride (NaCl)

  • Overall reaction :

    2NaCl(l) electrolysis → 2NaCl(l) + Cl 2(g)

  • At the Cathode (reduction) :

    Na + + e - → Na(l)

  • At the Anode (oxidation) :

    2Cl - → Cl 2(g) +2e -
