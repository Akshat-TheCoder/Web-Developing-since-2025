Electrolysis of water

  • Overall reaction: 2H2O(l)  electrolysis → 2H2(g) + O2(g)
  • At the cathode (reduction): 2H2O(l) + 2e− → H2(g) + 2OH−(aq)
  • At the anode (oxidation): 2H2O(l) → O2(g) + 4H+(aq) + 4e− (in acidic solution)

Electrolysis of molten sodium chloride (NaCl)

  • Overall reaction: 2NaCl(l)  electrolysis → 2Na(l) + Cl2(g)
  • At the cathode (reduction): Na+ + e− → Na(l)
  • At the anode (oxidation): 2Cl− → Cl2(g) + 2e−
